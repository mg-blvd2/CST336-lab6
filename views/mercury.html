Welcome to Mercury

Mercury is the first planet in our solar system
and the closest to the sun. It is also the smallest
planet in our solar system. It has no moons. Its orbit
Is much shorter than the Earth's; it only lasts 88 days.

It was discovered in 14th century BC by Assyrian
Astronomers.
